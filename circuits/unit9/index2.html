We are a ten-acre, diversified farm in scenic Vermilion County, Illinois. We sell to restaurants, breweries, grocers, and individuals in the Central Illinois region. Our farm is always growing and changing, and we are excited for what the 2015 season will bring!


We began our farm journey in 2011 with an empty piece of land and a few hand tools. Our first planting was our beloved hop yard, built with locust trees from our property. These trees have giant, gnarly thorns and, well, they're awesome. It seemed only fitting they should be our namesake; Big Thorn Farm was born.


After the hops came our blackberries, raspberries, grapes, asparagus, gooseberries, and fruit trees. Then the herbs, sunchokes, walking onions, crosnes, and shiitake mushrooms made their debut. We enjoy plants that cycle seamlessly though the seasons, blossoming and hibernating like the humans who tend them. Every year we add a few new things, and every year our perennial oasis continues to grow!


This past year, we hand-built our own tiny house and moved permanently to the farm. We live simply, off-grid, and couldn't be happier. Sometimes you don't realize your dream is coming true until you're actually living it.


Blackberries: Late June-July Raspberries: Late August-September Hops: 7 varieties from August-September Sunchokes: White, Red, and Pink, October-March Crosnes: Fall Walking Onions: Year-round Potato Onions: Fall Horseradish: Late Fall Shiitake Mushrooms: Spring & Fall, dependent on flushes Asparagus: Early Spring Fresh Herbs: Sage, Thyme, Lemon Thyme, Lemon Balm, Chocolate Mint, Orange Mint, Apple Mint, Garlic Chives Baby Ginger: Unavailable for 2015
