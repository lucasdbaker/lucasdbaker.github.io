What is Big Thorn Farm?

We are a ten-acre, diversified farm in scenic Vermilion County, Illinois. We sell to restaurants, breweries, grocers, and individuals in the Central Illinois region. Our farm is always growing and changing, and we are excited for what the 2015 season will bring!
