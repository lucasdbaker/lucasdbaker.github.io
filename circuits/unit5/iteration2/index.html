Featured Cocktail of the Week

The Pandar

  • 2oz Absolut Mandarin
  • 2oz Midori
  • 1oz Captain Morgan
  • 1oz Malibu
  • 2oz pineapple juice

Shake ingredients very well.
Strain into martini glass or over rocks.
Garnish with a lime.
